Bamboo-Steamed Fish

Bamboo-steamed fish is a traditional culinary technique in certain Asian cuisines. Fresh fish, often whole or in fillets, is delicately wrapped in bamboo leaves or placed inside hollow bamboo tubes. These natural vessels are then used to steam the fish, infusing it with the subtle fragrance of bamboo and aromatic herbs. The result is a tender, flavorful, and visually appealing dish.
